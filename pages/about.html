What is MyPFE?

Make Your Professional Future Efficient 14.0 is the leading job fair of the Faculty of Sciences of Sfax, organized by the Computer Science and Communications Department in partnership with IEEE FSS Student Branch, CPC FSS, and GDG on Campus.

Over the years, it has become a trusted meeting point between students, industry leaders, and top organizations. This edition brings together company booths showcasing career opportunities, internships, and innovative projects tailored to students' ambitions.

Who Are We?

A collaboration of leading academic and professional organizations

Faculty of Sciences of Sfax

Founded in 1987

Established in 1987, the Faculty of Sciences of Sfax (FSS), under the University of Sfax, is a leading institution in Tunisia for higher education and research. Located on Soukra Road, just 4 km from the city center, FSS offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Computer Science.

3500+ Students
435+ Professors

IEEE FSS Student Branch

Founded in 2018

Founded in 2018, IEEE FSS SB is a vibrant community with over 250 members and 13 active chapters. We connect the university with the professional world, bridging academic knowledge and real-world experience. Our mission is to foster innovation and entrepreneurship through projects, competitions, and workshops.

250+ Members
13 Chapters
2500+ Social Media Followers

Competitive Programming Club (CPC)

Established in 2022

The Competitive Programming Club (CPC) at the Faculty of Sciences of Sfax was established in 2022 to cultivate algorithmic excellence and competitive programming culture. Our mission is to develop world-class competitive programmers while fostering a collaborative learning environment that bridges academic theory with industry-demanded problem-solving skills.

National Competition Participants Industry-Ready Problem Solving 3+ Years of Excellence

GDG on Campus FSS

Google Developer Groups

Google Developer Groups On Campus is a university-based community group for students interested in Google development technologies. It is a place where students can grow as developers, from beginner to advanced levels, while learning alongside peers who share the same passion.
