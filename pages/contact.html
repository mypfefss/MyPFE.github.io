Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MyPFE 14.0

Is registration really free for students?

Yes! Student registration is completely free and includes access to all workshops, networking sessions, lunch, and a welcome pack.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your student ID, copies of your CV, a notebook for taking notes, and your laptop for hands-on workshops.

Are the workshops suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer workshops for all skill levels, from beginner to advanced. Each workshop clearly indicates its difficulty level.

Can I attend if I'm not from FSS?

Absolutely! MyPFE 14.0 is open to all students and recent graduates, regardless of their university or institution.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes! All registered participants will receive a certificate of attendance, and workshop participants will get specialized certificates.

How can companies participate?

Companies can participate through our partnership packages (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond). Visit our Partners page for detailed information.
