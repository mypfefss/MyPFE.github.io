About MyPFE 14.0

Bridging the gap between academic excellence and professional success

Make Your Professional Future Efficient 14.0 is the leading job fair of the Faculty of Sciences of Sfax, organized by the Computer Science and Communications Department in partnership with IEEE FSS Student Branch, CPC FSS, and GDG on Campus.

Over the years, it has become a trusted meeting point between students, industry leaders, and top organizations, offering career opportunities, internships, and innovative projects tailored to students' ambitions.
